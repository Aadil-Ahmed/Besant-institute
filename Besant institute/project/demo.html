Facts about coffee !

cappuccino

A cappuccino is an espresso-based coffee drink that originated in Austria and was later popularized in Italy and is prepared with steamed milk foam

Americano

Caffè Americano is a type of coffee drink prepared by diluting an espresso with hot water, giving it a similar strength to traditionally brewed coffee

Cortado

A cortado is a beverage consisting of espresso mixed with a roughly equal amount of warm milk to reduce the acidity.



Mocha

Deliciously sweet, nutty and chocolatey. This creamy coffee drink is an espresso-based beverage but it often causes confusion as to exactly what a mocha is.

Macchiato

Caffè macchiato, sometimes called espresso macchiato, is an espresso coffee drink with a small amount of milk, usually foamed.


Irish Coff

Irish coffee is a caffeinated alcoholic drink consisting of Irish whiskey, hot coffee and sugar, which has been stirred and topped with cream.


Pan Cake

A pancake is a flat cake, prepared from a starch-based batter that may contain eggs,often frying with oil or butter. It is a type of batter bread

FrenchToast

French toast is a dish of sliced bread soaked in beaten eggs and often milk or cream, then pan fried. Alternative names are "eggy bread", "gypsy toast", and "poor knights".

Waffle

A waffle is a dish made from leavened batter or dough that is cooked between two plates that are patterned to give a impression.


Fruit Bowl

Fruit bowls are primarily meant to offer convenient storage for your produce while keeping it fresh for longer.



Spanish Egg

The traditional Spanish omelette does not have onion, just extra virgin olive oil, potatoes, eggs and salt.



ScrambleEgg

Scrambled eggs is a dish made from eggs stirred, butter, oil, and sometimes other ingredients, and heated so that they form into curds
